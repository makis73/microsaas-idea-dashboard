# Product Hunt Pitfall Helper

## Overview
Tool that helps indie makers and startups avoid common pitfalls when launching on Product Hunt. Focus on traffic spike handling, server scaling, and launch preparation with actionable checklists and monitoring.

## Problem Statement
- Indie makers get featured on Product Hunt but servers crash from traffic
- No centralized resource for launch preparation
- Teams waste time fixing issues during critical launch period
- Experience gap between first-time and experienced hunters
- Post-launch analytics scattered across tools

## Solution
- Launch preparation checklist (technical, marketing, content)
- Server load testing simulation
- Real-time monitoring during launch
- Post-launch analytics dashboard
- Community knowledge base (what worked for others)
- Template gallery (hunt pages, tweets, emails)

## Target Market
- **Primary:** Indie hackers and solo founders
- **Secondary:** Early-stage startups
- **Tertiary:** Marketing agencies helping with launches
- **Market Size:** 1,000+ Product Hunt launches monthly

## Technical Stack
### MVP (Week 2)
- **Frontend:** Next.js/React (checklist app)
- **Backend:** Node.js/Python for monitoring
- **Testing:** Load testing simulations
- **Database:** PostgreSQL for launch data

## Risks & Mitigation
### Technical Risks
- **Load testing accuracy:** Use realistic simulations, not just synthetic
- **Monitoring reliability:** Multiple monitoring locations

### Market Risks
- **Niche market:** Focus on depth, not breadth
- **Seasonal launches:** Steady stream of new products

### Operational Risks
- **Knowledge maintenance:** Keep up with Product Hunt changes
- **Customer support:** Comprehensive documentation, community help
