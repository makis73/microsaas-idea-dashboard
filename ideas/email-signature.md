# SIGNATURE PRO - Email Signature Generator

## Overview
A simple tool that creates professional email signatures in 2 clicks. Perfect for professionals who want to look polished without HTML/CSS skills.

## Problem Statement
- Professionals waste time creating email signatures manually
- Existing solutions are either too complex or too expensive
- Most people don't have HTML/CSS skills to create professional signatures
- Signatures are inconsistent across teams and companies

## Solution
- Simple web form to input personal/professional details
- Multiple professional templates to choose from
- Instant preview of signature
- Download as HTML, PNG, or PDF
- Copy-paste ready for Gmail, Outlook, Apple Mail

## Target Market
- **Primary:** Professionals (sales, marketing, executives, freelancers)
- **Secondary:** Small businesses and teams
- **Market Size:** 500,000+ professionals in Greece, 4+ billion email users worldwide

## Technical Stack
### MVP (Week 1)
- **Frontend:** Flutter Web or React (simple form + preview)
- **Backend:** Python (FastAPI/Flask) or Node.js
- **PDF Generation:** WeasyPrint or html2pdf
- **Database:** SQLite (local) or PostgreSQL
- **Hosting:** Vercel, Railway, or simple VPS

### Future Features
- Team accounts with branding
- Analytics (signature opens, clicks)
- API access for developers
- Integration with CRM systems
- Bulk signature generation

## Monetization

## Risks & Mitigation
### Technical Risks
- **PDF generation issues:** Use proven libraries (WeasyPrint)
- **Email client compatibility:** Test across major clients (Gmail, Outlook, Apple Mail)

### Market Risks
- **Low willingness to pay:** Start with very affordable pricing
- **Competition:** Focus on simplicity and Greek market

### Operational Risks
- **Solo developer burnout:** Keep scope small, iterate based on feedback
- **Customer support:** Use FAQ, automated responses
