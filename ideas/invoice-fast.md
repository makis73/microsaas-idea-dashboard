# INVOICE FAST - Simple Invoice Generator

## Overview
Basic invoice generator for freelancers and small businesses. No complex accounting features, just clean, professional invoices that get you paid faster.

## Problem Statement
- Freelancers waste time creating invoices manually in Word/Excel
- Existing accounting software is too complex and expensive
- Small businesses need simple invoicing, not full accounting
- Professional invoices improve payment speed
- No simple, affordable solution for Greek freelancers

## Solution
- Clean, professional invoice templates
- Simple form to input client and project details
- Automatic calculations (VAT, totals)
- Download as PDF or send via email
- Invoice tracking (sent, viewed, paid)
- Basic client management

## Target Market
- **Primary:** Freelancers (designers, developers, consultants)
- **Secondary:** Small businesses (1-5 employees)
- **Tertiary:** Service providers (plumbers, electricians, tutors)
- **Market Size:** 300,000+ freelancers in Greece, 70M+ worldwide

### MVP (Week 1)

- **PDF Generation:** ReportLab or WeasyPrint

## Risks & Mitigation
### Technical Risks
- **PDF formatting issues:** Use proven libraries, extensive testing
- **Data security:** Implement proper encryption, regular backups

### Market Risks
- **Price sensitivity:** Start low, increase as value proven
- **Feature creep:** Stay focused on core invoicing

### Operational Risks
- **Payment delays:** Email reminders, late fee options
- **Tax compliance:** Consult with Greek accountant for VAT rules
